Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID and any medical information relevant to your treatment. We provide all necessary robes, slippers, and amenities. You may want to bring comfortable clothing to change into after your treatment.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows you to complete any necessary paperwork, change, and begin your relaxation experience without feeling rushed.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a 50% fee. No-shows will be charged the full amount of the scheduled service.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes, we offer beautifully packaged gift certificates that can be purchased for any amount or specific services. They can be purchased online or at our reception desk and never expire.

Are your products cruelty-free and sustainable?

Absolutely. We are committed to using only cruelty-free, vegan, and sustainably sourced products. Our suppliers are carefully selected based on their ethical practices and environmental consciousness.
